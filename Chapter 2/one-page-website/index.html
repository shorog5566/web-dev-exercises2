Plot Overview

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a thief who steals secrets from people's dreams. He is hired to plant an idea (an "inception") into a target's mind. The team enters multiple dream layers; reality and dreams blur as personal conflicts surface.

Key Information

  • Director: Christopher Nolan
  • Release: July 16, 2010
  • Runtime: 148 minutes
  • Genre: Sci-Fi / Action / Thriller
  • Budget: $160 million

Cast

  • Leonardo DiCaprio — Dom Cobb
  • Marion Cotillard — Mal Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt — Arthur
  • Tom Hardy — Eames

Characters

Dom Cobb: extractor haunted by loss. Ariadne: architect who builds dream levels. Arthur: planner and partner.

Reviews

Critical acclaim for originality, visual effects and score. IMDb 8.8/10 — Rotten Tomatoes 87%.
