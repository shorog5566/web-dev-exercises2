Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a simple Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pork. It is easy to make and tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Method

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the spaghetti until it is soft.
  2. Cook the diced meat in a pan until it becomes crispy.
  3. In a bowl, mix eggs, cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Keep some pasta water and drain the spaghetti.
  5. Put the pasta in the pan with the meat and remove from heat.
  6. Pour the egg mixture on the pasta and mix quickly.
  7. Serve the pasta on plates and add parsley if you want.

Enjoy your meal!
